JamSai Foundation - A Smile on Every Face

The families of Jammeh and Saidy Kunda have come together to create the JamSai Foundation with the intention of leaving a lasting positive impact on society. We have recognised the unmet social needs within our communities and see the opportunity to contribute our part.

JamSai Foundation is a charitable non-profit organisation dedicated to providing grants and donations, both in kind and cash, to less privileged individuals. The foundation concentrates its efforts in rural areas, where we recognised the challenges faced by many, including limited access to income, water, and soap for clean clothing.

JamSai is determined to render aid and support services to vulnerable people in communities, including the needy, widows, orphans, and children in Quranic boarding schools. Moreover, we provide quality and nutritious meals during the holy month of Ramadan.

Our main goal is to contribute to the promotion of equality, equity, and provide opportunities for others to enjoy life's privileges.

Our Mission

"To be a model charitable foundation dedicated to empowering low-income families and individuals in need by providing essential resources—such as clean clothing, health support, educational materials—and promoting strong, healthy families. We achieve this through donation campaigns, fundraising efforts, volunteer programmes and community partnerships with a focus on creating lasting, positive change in the lives of those we serve."

Our Vision

To drive progress in education, health, agriculture, environmental sustainability, climate change awareness, and cultural and religious values. We also strive to foster teamwork, promote collaboration, and encourage the reuse of materials and clothing to help protect our planet and reduce landfill waste.

Our Current Programs

  • Cloth Donation Drive: Providing clean clothing is a direct and tangible way to meet immediate needs, especially for those experiencing hardship.
  • School Supply Drive: Supporting education by providing school supplies, helping students with tools for success.
  • Fundraising Campaign: Sustaining our programmes through fundraising to cover operational costs and expand initiatives.

Our Achievements

  • December 2023: 10 households in Darsimallah village benefited from clothing drive
  • March 2024: Distributed rice and sugar to 25 households during Ramadan
  • March 2024: 100 people in Chugen Fula and Chugen Misera communities received clothing and accessories
  • June 2024: 156 people in three communities received clothing donations
  • October 2024: Distributed D21,000 ($300) worth of school supplies to 90 pupils
  • Raised over D420,000 ($6,000) for medical surgery expenses
